## Goal This Week
Reconnect with the geometric intuition behind vectors and matrices.
Understand how matrices can be "broken apart" — this is the foundation for PCA, SVD, and neural networks.

---

## Key Concepts to Master

### Chapter 3 — Analytic Geometry
- [ ] Norm: what it means to "measure" a vector
- [ ] Inner product: angle and similarity between vectors
- [ ] Orthogonality and orthonormal basis
- [ ] Orthogonal projections (used everywhere in ML)
- [ ] Lengths and distances in vector spaces

### Chapter 4 — Matrix Decomposition
- [ ] Determinant and Trace — what they tell you about a matrix
- [ ] Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors — directions a matrix "stretches"
- [ ] Eigendecomposition and Diagonalization
- [ ] Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) — the most important decomposition in ML
- [ ] Matrix Approximation using SVD
